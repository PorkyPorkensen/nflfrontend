SportSync Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SportSync ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SportSync mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide, such as email address, display name, and authentication data.
  • Technical and usage information, such as app interactions, device identifiers, and diagnostics generated by analytics/auth providers.
  • Data needed to provide app features, including bracket and profile-related data submitted in the app.

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you create an account, sign in, or update profile details.
  • Automatically through integrated services such as Firebase Authentication and Firebase Analytics.
  • Through API requests made to our backend services to support app functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts and authenticate users.
  • To operate core app functionality and sync user data.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To detect abuse, maintain security, and comply with legal requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers that process data on our behalf, such as Firebase (Google), only to provide and support the app.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

  • You can update certain account details within the app.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us at the email below.
  • You can stop all collection by uninstalling the app.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

SportSync is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and revise the effective date when changes are made.
